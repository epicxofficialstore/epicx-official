Privacy Policy

At EpicX, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, Email address, Phone number, Game/User IDs (e.g., MLBB ID), Instagram usernames
  • Payment Information: We may collect payment details to process transactions. All payments are handled securely through trusted third-party gateways.
  • Technical Data: IP address, Browser type, Device information, Pages visited on our site

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your recharge or social media order
  • Communicate with you (order confirmation, support, etc.)
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Prevent fraud or abuse

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., payment gateways, delivery partners)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track website performance. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
