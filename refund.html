EpicX - Refund Policy

Refund Policy

At EpicX, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and reliably. However, in some rare cases, due to technical issues, server delays, or third-party system downtime, your order may be delayed.

If your order is not processed or fulfilled within 7 days of successful payment due to such issues, you will be eligible for a full refund.

Refund Terms:

  • Refunds are only applicable if the delay is caused by technical issues and the order remains incomplete after 7 days.
  • To request a refund, please provide valid payment proof and your order details.
  • Refunds will not be issued for incorrect information submitted by the user (such as wrong Player ID or Username).
  • Once approved, refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 3–7 business days.

We appreciate your understanding and patience in the rare event of any technical difficulties.
